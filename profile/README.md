## Overview

SketchBook Pro Advanced is a professional digital sketching and illustration application that provides a natural brush engine, pressure-sensitive drawing tools, and a distraction-free canvas designed to feel like drawing on paper rather than operating software. It prioritizes the drawing experience above everything else, keeping tools accessible but unobtrusive so that the creative process flows naturally. The application serves concept artists, illustrators, industrial designers, and anyone who sketches as part of their creative process.

The brush engine simulates pencils, pens, markers, airbrushes, and paint with pressure and tilt sensitivity that responds naturally to stylus input, producing strokes that look and feel like their physical counterparts. The brush library covers the range from technical pencils through expressive paint brushes, and custom brushes extend the built-in set. Brush response curves are tunable for individual preference.

The distraction-free interface hides tools until needed, maximizing canvas space and minimizing visual clutter that interrupts the creative flow. The minimal UI philosophy treats the drawing experience as primary and tool access as secondary. Full-screen mode eliminates every interface element except the canvas.

Perspective guides, symmetry tools, and rulers provide structural assistance for technical and architectural sketching without imposing rigid constraints on freeform drawing. The predictive stroke engine smooths hand-drawn lines into clean curves when desired. Layers with blend modes enable non-destructive composition.

For artists and designers who want a digital drawing tool that prioritizes the feel of drawing over feature complexity, SketchBook Pro Advanced is the illustration app that makes the canvas feel like paper and the brush feel like a physical tool.

## Performance

SketchBook Pro balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your system responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series chips, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
